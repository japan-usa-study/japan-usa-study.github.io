Study in the USA

Background

Are you a Japanese student dreaming of completing your graduate studies in the United States? You're in the right place! Our platform, created by Michigan State University and funded by a grant from the U.S. Embassy Tokyo, is designed to guide you through every step of your journey—from choosing the right university to understanding cultural differences and adjusting to life abroad.

Engage in discussion forums with other prospective students, explore scholarship opportunities, and get practical tips on applications, visas, housing, and more.

Your dream of studying in the USA starts here. Let us help you make it a reality!
