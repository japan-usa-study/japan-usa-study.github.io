University Selection

Find the perfect university for your academic journey

Choosing the right university is one of the most important steps in your study abroad journey. Here are some ideas to help you get started. Whether you're looking for academic excellence, a vibrant campus life, or affordability, this guide will point you in the right direction.

How to Choose a University

  • 1Research Your Field of Study: Does the university have strong programs in your area of interest?
  • 2Explore Faculty: Can you contact any faculty that can give you better insight into your field of study? Establishing these early connections can be extremely advantageous to your academic career.
  • 3Location Matters: Big city or small town? East Coast or West Coast? Climate and culture vary greatly across the U.S.
  • 4Tuition and Scholarships: Compare costs and explore financial aid options.
  • 5Social Life: Are there any student organizations or campus activities that are of interest to you?

Resources for University Selection

  • U.S. News Best Colleges Rankings - Compare universities by rankings, tuition, and programs.
  • EducationUSA - The official source for U.S. higher education guidance.
  • International Student - Filter for schools by major, location, and type of degree.
  • Peterson's - College admittance exam preparation site.
